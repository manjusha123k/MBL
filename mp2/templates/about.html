About Me

Who Am I I am a online Mobile Price Predicting tool developed using a machine learning algorithm.
Why Am I To fix an effective price for a product, the company have to depend on the past data and predict a good price for the product. And in this step of prediction I can help the companies to give a second opinion in fraction of seconds.
How I Differ From Others May so many developers designed online mobile price predictors, all they are only stick with one dataset, which can be get older in coming days. But I can learn the and predict the best price for you by considering the modern dataset from you.
Who Developed Me I am developed by a team of students(Sk.Subhan Saheb, K.Jyothshna Devi, K.Madhuri, Ch.Sai Nirmal) under guidence of M.S.Kumar Reddy(Asst. Professor),CSE Department, Sri Vasavi Engineering College.
